The Envision Center is a lab committed to providing novel solutions for effectively communicating complex research concepts through computer graphics, advanced visualization, auditory (sound), haptic (touch), and multimodal interaction.

As a division of RCAC, their creative technology integrates with state-of-the-art high performance computation to assist researchers, instructors, and leaders in their quest for new knowledge and innovative products.
